In Michigan

Ann Arbor Huron River Watertrail Argo Park Cascades

Springtime in Ann Arbor is a beautiful time. Even though it can be chilly at times,
it is nice to walk my dog Bacon. This site includes some pictures I have taken on these walks.
Sometimes we walk around the neighborhood, other times we make it to the woods.
Ann Arbor also has some beautiful lakes and rivers including
the Huron River Watertrail and the Argo Park Cascades.
